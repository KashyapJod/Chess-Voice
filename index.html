Voice Chess Instructions

Voice Commands:

  • "e2 to e4" - Move from square to square (recommended)
  • "Knight to f3" - Move piece to destination
  • "Castle kingside" or "Castle queenside" - Castling moves

Click the Speak Move button and say your move clearly.

For best results, always specify the starting and ending squares.
